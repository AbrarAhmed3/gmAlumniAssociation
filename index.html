About Us

GMAA

Gour Mahavidyalaya Alumni Association is a registered association under the west Bengal Societies Registration act 1961, Registration Number S0009598 of 2019-2020. The alumni association has been participating and contributing significantly in several activities of the college. The esteemed alumni of our college have contributed A water Plant to solve drinking water crisis of Gour Mahavidyalaya. Apart from organizing the yearly reunion, they also involve actively in conducting and managing the college's several observation day and other cultural programmes. Moreover, the association is quite intent on maintaining the pleasant natural ambience of the college. They regularly participate in the tree plantation programmes that occur several times in a year and nurture the saplings and plants to sustain the lush greenery of the college campus, and conserve the college's pond to the college.With the hope of connecting all of our esteemed alumnus, we sincerely request to every alumni of Gour Mahavidyalaya to be a member of our association and maintain this pleasant connection lifelong.
